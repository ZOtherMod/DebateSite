How DebateHub Works

1

Create Your Account

Sign up and create your profile to start your debating journey. Every new user begins with a base MMR rating.

2

Join the Queue

Enter the matchmaking system and get paired with an opponent of similar skill level. The system ensures balanced matches.

3

Engage in Debate

Take turns presenting arguments within time limits. One person argues for the proposition, the other for the opposition.

4

Grow Your Skills

Win or lose, you'll gain MMR based on performance. Review your debates and learn from each experience.
